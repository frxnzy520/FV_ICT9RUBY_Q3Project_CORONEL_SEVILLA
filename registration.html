About Maxi Taxi

Maxi Taxi is a travel service for women and women only. That means no adult men are allowed to ride! Sorry, not sorry.

Our taxi service provides booking online and in real life. For onsite booking, just go to your nearest Maxi Taxi stall (usually at malls) and let the staff guide you. For online booking, go to our website or dowload the app (for extra perks) and click book a ride.

If you're a man reading this, don't worry! You can still book our taxis but on behalf of a woman. This can help your daughters, sisters, and other family get home safely if you're not able to accompany them.

Why choose Maxi Taxi?


Compared to other ride services, our rules, regulations, and safety policies are stricter, making sure that all our passengers will arrive home wihtout a sweat. It is also inclusive, with our vehicles having wheelchair options, bigger or smaller seatbelts,adjustable seating, etc. Just make sure to check the options you ned when booking a ride!

Our Purpose


Maxi Taxi ensures than women and children will be safe from danger. This includes sexual assault, rape, drugging, stalking, etc. It's to prevent anything bad happening to you and your loved ones. We also aim to make a space where women will feel safe and calm.
